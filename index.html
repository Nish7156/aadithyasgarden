ABOUT US

Microgreens are Vegetable greens that are approximately 1-3 inches tall and is the next stage of sprouts. Greens are harvested 7-15 days after sowing the seeds, its stem and leaves are considered edible. They are treasure in Potassium, Iron, Zinc, Magnesium, Copper and also a great source of Vitamins, Minerals, Antioxidants, High-Level of Beta -Carotenes, Fatty acids, amino acides etc. upto 40 times higher than those recorded mature Leaves.

MICRO GREENS Introduction

Health Benefits of Micro Greens?

Microgreens reduces the risk of Heart Diseases, Diabetics, Alzheimer’s & Certain Cancers. It is Good for Bone Health, Body Immunity, Eye Health, Hair Growth, Anti-Aging and it is a good food for weight loss.

How to consume Micro Greens ?

It is always recommended to eat the Microgreens raw to get the complete nutrients present in the greens. Add them to Your Salads, Soups, Veg & Non veg Curries (Just Before Serving) etc.
